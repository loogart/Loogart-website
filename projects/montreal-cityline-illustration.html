Montréal CityLine

In the Summer of 2013, one of my best friends moved into his brand new apartment and was looking to add a piece of art that would represent our favourite city, Montreal. He contacted me and asked me to design this illustration which would be prominently hung in his living room. I agreed and asked that he provide me with landmarks and elements that he considered Montreal-worthy.

I created this colorful piece which is exactly what Montreal is about. This illustration defines a huge part of me, my style as an artist and is licensed for apparel production by Kalooba, a sister company of mine.

Simplicity of the CityLine

The style presents bold lines married to circular anchors that hold the design together. Since the creation of the Montreal design, I have illustrated CityLines for several other cities such as Toronto and Ottawa. I also created one for Canada.
